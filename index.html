ABOUT IEEE

IEEE is the world’slargest professional association dedicated to advancing technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity. IEEE and its members inspire a global community through its highly cited publication, conferences, technology standards, and professional and educational activities. It has more than 426,000 members in more than 160 countries. IEEE was formed in 1963 after a merger between the Institute of Radio Engineers(IRE), founded in 1912, and American Institute of Electrical Engineers(AIEE). IEEE is now the leading authority on a range of technology including aero space system, computers, telecommunication, biomedical engineering, electric power,consumer electronics and many more. The IEEE serves more than to just provide a networking platform for professionals. It sponsors more than 1,600 conferences in 91 countries annually to aid the progression to technology. With 2,669 student branches at colleges and universities in 80 countries, it has a membership population of nearly 117,000 students.

ABOUT THM
